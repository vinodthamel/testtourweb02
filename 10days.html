Ten Days Tour

A Journey Through Heritage, Nature, and Coastal Beauty

Embark on an unforgettable 10-day journey through Sri Lanka, where ancient history, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant culture converge. From the iconic rock fortress of Sigiriya and the sacred caves of Dambulla to the lush greenery of Ella and the tranquil beaches of Mirissa, this tour offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. Explore the historic cities of Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa, wander through the charming streets of Galle, and immerse yourself in the rich heritage of Kandy. Experience the diverse beauty of Sri Lanka, from the wildlife in Udawalawa to the bustling capital of Colombo.

Highlights

  • Dambulla Cave Temple
  • Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Matale Spice Garden
  • Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic (Kandy)
  • Kandy Cultural Show
  • Kandy to Ella Scenic Train Ride
  • Nine Arch Bridge
  • Little Adam’s Peak
  • Ravana Falls
  • Yala National Park Safari
